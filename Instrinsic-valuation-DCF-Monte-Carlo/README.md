# DCF Valuation using Monte Carlo Simulation

## Overview
Built a discounted cash flow (DCF) valuation model and extended it using Monte Carlo simulation to account for uncertainty in growth and discount rates.

## Key Features
- DCF-based intrinsic value calculation
- Monte Carlo simulation for probabilistic valuation
- Sensitivity analysis across growth and discount rates
- Visualization of valuation distribution

## Results
- Intrinsic value range: ₹1600 – ₹3050
- Market price: ₹5700
- Probability of undervaluation: ~0%
- Mean intrinsic value: ~₹3100

## Conclusion
Under conservative to moderate assumptions, the stock appears overvalued, suggesting market expectations may be higher than modeled growth and risk assumptions.
